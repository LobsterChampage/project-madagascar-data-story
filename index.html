The Elon Effect

How does Elon Musk impact

By: A. K. Elwakil, F. S. Ytteborg, F. A. Hægermark, S. Jaoua

About

There is no doubt that comments about companies made by important people could have an impact on these companies in different ways. It might even have an impact on the stock prices of these businesses. One of the people who have gained perhaps the most media attention for his tweets and statements regarding different companies is the entrepreneur and businessman behind the likes of Tesla and SpaceX, Elon Musk.

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer.

Not so long ago Elon Musk tweeted that Tesla will stop accepting Bitcoin as a method of payment. Shortly after the bitcoin price dropped dramatically. A coincidence? It is these types of questions we would look closer at throughout this data story. We will look at how Elon Musk’s quotes impact the financial markets as well as the popularity of different companies. Is there an Elon Effect?
