By: A. K. Elwakil, F. S. Ytteborg, F. A. Hægermark, S. Jaoua

Introduction

There is no doubt that comments about companies made by important people could have impact these companies in different ways. It might even have an impact on the stock prices of these businesses. One of the people who have gained perhaps the most media attention for his tweets and statements regarding different companies is the entrepreneur and businessman behind the likes of Tesla and SpaceX, Elon Musk. Not so long ago Elon Musk tweeted that Tesla will stop accepting Bitcoin as a method of payment. Shortly after the bitcoin price dropped dramatically. A coincidence? It is these types of questions we would look closer at throughout this data story. We will look at how Elon Musk’s quotes impact the financial markets as well as the popularity of different companies. Is there an Elon Effect?

What is Quotebank?

Quotebank is an open corpus of more than 178 million quotations attributed to the speakers that uttered them. The quotes are extracted from 162 million English news articles that were published between 2008 and 2020. In this project we are focusing on the data between 2015 to 2020. In this particular data story we are going to extract and analyse the Elon Musk quotes within this time period.
